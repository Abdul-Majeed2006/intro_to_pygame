### How to Use PBR Textures
Each material usually includes:
- **Albedo/Diffuse** - Base color
- **Normal** - Fake surface detail
- **Metallic** - Metal vs. non-metal
- **Roughness** - Shiny vs. matte
- **AO (Ambient Occlusion)** - Shadows in crevices
